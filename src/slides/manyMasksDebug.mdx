# Layer guide

- <Color color="red">Red (edge reflection):</Color> outer blur/glow that creates perceived thickness.
- <Color color="orange">Orange (emboss reflection):</Color> sharper, shifted highlights that add perceived depth.
- <Color color="green">Green (refraction):</Color> a thin band that simulates light bending at the glass edge.
- <Color color="blue">Blue (blur):</Color> the softened, interior blur that diffuses the background.
- <Color color="purple">Purple (blend layers):</Color> subtle blending/filtering that modulates color and saturation.
- <Color color="yellow">Yellow (blend-edge):</Color> contrasty edge detail to separate glass from background.
- <Color color="cyan">Cyan (highlight):</Color> final bright rim that reads as a specular highlight.
