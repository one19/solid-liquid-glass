### Why those choices?

- Separate layers allow different filters per visual role
  - soft blur for background
  - stronger blur+color shifts for emboss,
  - subtle bright highlights for edges.
- Masks keep the inner content readable while letting visual artifacts (glow, refraction) sit just outside the content's edge.


```css
/* common technique used across layers */
backdrop-filter: blur(var(--extra-blur)) brightness(1.25);
mask: linear-gradient(white 0 0) padding-box, linear-gradient(white 0 0) content-box;
mask-composite: exclude, exclude; /* carve out inner content area */
```
